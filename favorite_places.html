About me

Throughout the years, I've traveled through the mysterious wodl with only a few hundred dollars in my wallet. I've heard stories about being careful when going to a third world country because people are poor and desperate, they'll rob you. You're American!! This wasn't the case for me, I actually found people living in poverty to be the most gracious and generous people I've met, ever!
